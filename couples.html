⬡ Third-eye perspective

When this is enabled, Brodonam will occasionally pause the film and offer a perspective from outside — like a trusted friend watching the both of you watch the film. This creates a shared reference point that's easier to discuss than direct confrontation.

Third-eye mode on
Discussion prompts Brodonam will offer during the film
"What did you notice in yourself when that scene happened? Not what you thought — what did you feel?"
"If the character who just spoke was saying something to you — not to the other character — what were they saying?"
"Is there anything in this film that feels uncomfortably familiar? You don't have to share it — just notice it."
How it works
01Both of you watch the same film in sync — wherever you are
02At key moments, Brodonam pauses and offers you each a private insight, based on what you shared individually
03After each insight, you can optionally share it with your partner — or keep it private
04At the end, Brodonam surfaces a joint reflection — things both of you resonated with — and offers discussion questions
05You each leave with your own action points, and one or two you chose to do together
